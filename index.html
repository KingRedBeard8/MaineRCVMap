About The Maine Maproom

Maine politics through maps, data, and campaign analysis.

The Maine Maproom is a home for understanding Maine politics through maps, data, and campaign analysis. We look at elections geographically, historically, and strategically — not just who won, but where support came from, how coalitions moved, and what the results reveal about political power in Maine.
