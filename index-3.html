About This Project

These maps visualize Maine's ranked-choice voting elections at the town level, showing how ballots transfer between candidates as rounds progress. Data is derived from official cast vote records (CVRs) published by the Maine Secretary of State.

The 2018 CD2 primary data is based on a ~2.4% CVR sample of 1,243 ballots, scaled to official district totals. Intermediate rounds are estimated from sample proportions. The 2026 data will use the full official CVR once released post-certification.
