Maine Election Analysis

Ranked-Choice Voting
Round by Round

Interactive maps that step through Maine RCV results as ballots redistribute — watch towns shift color each round and follow the tabulation as it resolves.

How it works
Each map is driven by the Maine SOS cast vote record — real ballot-level data processed round by round, not just summary totals.
Controls
Click round nodes or use ← → arrow keys to step through tabulation. Drag the scrubber to sweep across rounds. Hover any town for a breakdown.
Data source
Official cast vote records from the Maine Secretary of State. District totals validated against certified SOS results.
